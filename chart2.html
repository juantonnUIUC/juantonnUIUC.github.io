Revenue per Player vs. Average Salary in American Professional Sports Leagues

This visualization compares revenue per player against the average salary in prominent American professional sports leagues. This interactive scatter plot enables you to explore the financial landscape of the Women's National Basketball Association (WNBA), National Basketball Association (NBA), Major League Soccer (MLS), National Hockey League (NHL), Major League Baseball (MLB), and National Football League (NFL).

Chart Overview:

The scatter plot juxtaposes the "Revenue per Player" and "Average Salary" metrics for each league. By default, the WNBA data point is shown, representing the league's unique position within the sports industry. However, users can choose to display additional leagues by selecting them from checkboxes provided.

Purpose:

This visualization is intended to shed light on the financial dynamics across different professional sports leagues in the United States.

How to Use:

  1. Toggle League Selection: Use the checkboxes to select one or more leagues you wish to compare on the scatter plot. By default, the WNBA point is shown as a starting point for comparison.
  2. Analyze the Data: Once you've selected your preferred leagues, the scatter plot will display the respective data points, each representing a league. You can mouseover any of the points to see detailed information.
